My story

From sketching superheroes on the back of my schoolbooks to crafting digital experiences that (hopefully) don’t crash my journey has always been fueled by creativity, curiosity, and a bit of caffeine.

I once took an outdated ERP system, gave it a full UI/UX makeover, and turned it into a sleek, modern mobile app. Think of it as turning a potato into french fries same core, way more appealing.

What do I do?

I specialize in UI/UX Design in the world of Interactive Media, blending beautiful visuals with smooth functionality. Think of me as the middle ground between a pixel perfectionist and a logic-loving developer.

How did I get here?

Started with Microsoft Paint, ended up designing mobile apps, websites, ERP systems, and SaaS products. Somewhere in between, I also dabbled in digital marketing, which taught me how to make things not just look good — but sell too.

Outside the screen?

I’m a team player, a tech geek, and once a competitive gamer during COVID (don’t worry, I’ve retired undefeated in snack consumption).
